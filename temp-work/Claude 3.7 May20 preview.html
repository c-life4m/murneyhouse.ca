Discover the Area

Explore the beauty and charm of our small town

Nestled in the heart of a picturesque small town, Murney House is the perfect base for exploring all that the region has to offer. From scenic hiking trails and waterfalls to charming local shops and restaurants, there's something for everyone just minutes from our doorstep.

Local Highlights

  • Historic downtown district (5 min walk)
  • Lakeside trails and beaches (10 min walk)
  • Local winery tours (15 min drive)
  • Farmer's market (Saturdays, 2 min walk)
  • Nature preserve with hiking trails (20 min drive)
